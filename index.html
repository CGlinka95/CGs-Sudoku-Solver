CG's Sudoku Solver, my dude

Instructions

  1. Click on a square to input a value between 1 and 9.
  2. Once the puzzle is complete, click the solve button.
  3. The application will take a few seconds to solve the puzzle.
  4. If the puzzle is invalid, a message will display below to say so.
  5. If the puzzle is valid, a success message will be displayed.
  6. To input a new puzzle, click the browser's refresh button.
  7. Enjoy, my dude!
